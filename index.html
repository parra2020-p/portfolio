ABOUT ANDRES

I Am just a human being passionate about social change and entrepreneurship. Truly believing that everybody can contribute make the world a better place. Focusing on the field of social innovation building capacity cross sectors with a focus on Entrepreneurship Development, Leadership Enabling, Sustainable Development, Exponential Technologies, Design Thinking and Liberating Structures. How to move forward the SDG indicators through Social Business, that's all our work about.

Also, along with the journey, starting different projects, and participating in different programs around the world. Successfully connected with the ecosystems in México, Chile, Hong Kong, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore and Bangladesh.

I've held leadership positions in the private, academic, a non-for-profit sectors. I'm also a member of 10+ social entrepreneurship international and national communities.

I'm always open to explore opportunities to collaborate and I'm currently available as facilitator, consultant and enabler for social ventures.

SPECIALITIES Social Business Development Social Innovation Management Design Thinking Implementation Business Model Innovation Leadership Development Change Management Sustainable Development

WHAT I'VE DONE

Since the beginning of my journey I knew I needed to do something more meaningful than just working for a company and making money.

Now, almost 5 years since I devoted my life to social business, I have focused on mentoring, consulting, training and research what makes social entrepreneurs thrive.

My work has expanded in more than 15 countries in 3 continents. With more emphasis in Asia and Latin America.
